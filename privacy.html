Privacy Policy

Pool data stays on your device. Scanner, checkout, and waitlist actions use the services needed to run them.

Effective Date: May 26, 2026

Short version: pool profiles, chemistry history, route notes, and checklist data stay in your browser on your device. If you join the Route Ready pilot list, we store your email. If you use the online AI scanner, your image is sent through our Cloudflare function to Anthropic for immediate analysis. If you upgrade PartSnap Pro, checkout is handled by Stripe. We also collect anonymous first-party launch events so we can see which free tools are being used.

1. Who We Are

SplashLens is a free, offline-first progressive web application (PWA) for pool service professionals, available at https://app.splashlens.com. This marketing website is available at https://splashlens.com. Both are operated by SplashLens. Contact: hello@splashlens.com.

2. Data We Do Not Collect By Default

SplashLens does not require an account and does not collect these by default:

  • Your name, phone number, or address
  • Account credentials (there is no account system)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to our servers
  • Any information about your customers or the pools you service

We collect your email only if you submit a waitlist or pilot form. We collect anonymous feature events such as page views, app opens, scan starts, upgrade clicks, and buy-link clicks to understand the launch funnel.

3. Your Pool Data - Stays on Your Device

All data you enter into SplashLens - pool profiles, volume measurements, chemical history, route stops, checklist progress - is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to SplashLens for account sync or backup
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Persists until you clear your browser data or uninstall the PWA

Because data lives only on your device, SplashLens cannot recover your data if you clear your browser storage or switch devices. We have no backup of anything you enter.

4. Cookies and Tracking

SplashLens does not use cookies for advertising or retargeting. We do not use fingerprinting. We run a small first-party event endpoint on splashlens.com to record anonymous launch activity such as page views, app opens, AI scan starts, upgrade clicks, and buy-link clicks. Event rows may include event name, page path, referrer, user agent, approximate country, and the small event properties needed to understand usage. These events do not include pool profiles, customer names, chemistry logs, route data, or submitted photos.

The app uses a service worker for offline caching - this caches app files locally on your device and is entirely standard PWA technology. It does not track behavior.

5. Cloudflare CDN

SplashLens is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and delivered via Cloudflare's content delivery network. When you access the app, your browser communicates with Cloudflare's servers to download app files and call our serverless functions. Cloudflare may process standard HTTP request metadata such as IP address, browser type, and request timestamp as part of normal CDN and security operation. This is governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

6. Online AI Scanner

SplashLens includes online AI scanner modes for equipment error displays, PartSnap part identification, and test strip field triage. Here is how scanner privacy works:

  • When you choose to scan equipment, the image is sent from your device to a Cloudflare Worker (a serverless function we operate)
  • That Worker makes a single API call to Anthropic's API to analyze the image
  • Images are not stored - by us or by Anthropic beyond the immediate API response processing
  • No image is retained, indexed, or used for any purpose other than returning the analysis to you
  • Use of the AI scanner is always opt-in - you must actively choose to take and submit a photo

Anthropic's data handling for API calls is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

7. Email, Checkout, and Payments

If you join the Route Ready pilot or another SplashLens email list, we store your email address and the source of the signup in our Cloudflare D1 database so we can contact you about that specific product or pilot. If you upgrade PartSnap Pro, payment checkout is handled by Stripe. We do not receive or store your full card number. Parts search links may open third-party sites; if an approved affiliate tag is configured later, SplashLens may earn a commission and will disclose that connection near those links.

8. Children's Privacy

SplashLens is a professional field reference tool intended for adult pool service technicians. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

To remove all SplashLens app data from your device, clear your browser's site data for app.splashlens.com or uninstall the PWA from your home screen. To request removal of a waitlist email, contact hello@splashlens.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we materially change this Privacy Policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We will not introduce data collection practices that contradict the core commitments in this policy without clear, prominent notice.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@splashlens.com
