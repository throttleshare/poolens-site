How It Works

Zero setup. Open it and go.

SplashLens is a PWA — a website that behaves like an app. No app store required, no account, no credit card.

1

Open app.splashlens.com in your browser

On any phone, tablet, or laptop. No download, no login. Just open the URL and you're in.

2

Add to your home screen (optional, takes 5 seconds)

iOS: Share → Add to Home Screen. Android: tap the browser menu → Install App. It'll look and feel like a native app — icon, full-screen, no browser chrome.

3

Save your pools once

Enter each pool on your route — name, volume, shape, filter type. They're saved to your device. Every subsequent visit, just pick the pool and the calculators pre-fill.

4

Use it offline all day

After the first load, SplashLens caches everything to your device. Pull the error code reference at a garage panel with no signal. Run the SLAM calculator in a basement equipment room. It just works.

Questions

FAQ

Is SplashLens really free? What's the catch?

No catch. SplashLens is free right now with no ads, no card, and no account required. The goal is field adoption first. Monetization comes later only after the tool proves daily value.

How does offline mode work?

When you first open SplashLens, the service worker caches the app, error code data, calculators, and reference tables to your device. Manual lookup and calculators work without signal after first load. AI camera scanning requires internet.

Does SplashLens compete with Skimmer or Pool Brain?

No. Those are service management platforms — billing, invoicing, customer CRM, route optimization. PoolBrain and Skimmer run the company. SplashLens saves the stop with code lookup, scanner, dosing, reports, and poolside reference. We intentionally don't do billing.

How do I install it on my phone?

Open app.splashlens.com in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android). On iOS: tap the Share icon → Add to Home Screen. On Android: tap the browser menu → Install App. Takes about 5 seconds. You'll get a home screen icon and full-screen mode — just like a native app, no App Store required.

What brands are covered in the error code library?

Hayward (TriStar, Super Pump, MasterTemp, OmniLogic), Pentair (IntelliFlow, IntelliFlo, MasterTemp, EasyTouch), Jandy (iAqualink, JXi), Zodiac, Maytronics/Dolphin (robot cleaners), Polaris, Kreepy Krauly, Waterway, and more. We're continuously adding codes — Beatbot and Betta robotic cleaners are in the current expansion.

What does the AI scanner actually do?

Error Scan, PartSnap, and Test Strip reader are live online. Error Scan attempts to read pool equipment displays and returns reference causes and fix steps. PartSnap suggests pool part IDs and search terms. Test Strip reader estimates readings for quick field triage. Without internet, use manual lookup and manual entry.

Is my pool data private?

Pool profiles, chemistry history, and route data live on your device in localStorage. AI scan images are sent to the scanner endpoint only when you use the online AI feature. There is no account or backend sync for your customer list.
